Our Heritage

A Living Legacy

Three and a half centuries of stories, now yours to write

Georgian Grandeur

Built in the 17th century during the reign of Charles II, Ash House Somerset stands as one of the finest examples of Queen Anne architecture in the West Country. Our limestone façade and original Georgian windows have witnessed centuries of English history.

Modern Renaissance

Today, we've carefully restored every period feature while seamlessly integrating contemporary luxury. From our authentic Finnish sauna to Egyptian cotton linens, every detail honors our heritage while embracing modern comfort.
